Free up disk space in a PPA
===========================

Step 1 — Remove obsolete packages
---------------------------------

Delete packages that are no longer needed or belong to an obsolete
series; see :ref:`package-deletion` for the deletion procedure and the
disk-pool mechanics that determine when freed space actually appears.

Step 2 — Request a larger quota
-------------------------------

To increase the PPA's quota beyond the 8 GiB default, file a question
with a written justification at https://answers.launchpad.net/launchpad.
A Launchpad administrator will consider the request and either grant
it or recommend an alternative approach.
